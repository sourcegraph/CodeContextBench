   - `CSSMathSum`, `CSSMathProduct`, `CSSMathMin`, `CSSMathMax`, `CSSMathClamp` classes
   - DOM-level APIs for CSS math operations

The crash likely occurs due to edge cases in the interaction between extreme value propagation during calc expression resolution and the specific handling of infinity/NaN in operations like mod() or rounding with infinite step values.
