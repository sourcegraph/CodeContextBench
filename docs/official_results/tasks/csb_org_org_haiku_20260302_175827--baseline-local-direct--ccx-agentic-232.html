## The Complete Architecture Chain:
The replication protocol works through a chain where:
1. **ReplicaFetcherThread** fetches records from the leader
2. **ReplicaManager** coordinates all replication operations
3. **Partition** manages ISR state and high watermarks
4. **LeaderEpochFileCache** ensures correct epoch-based recovery
5. **UnifiedLog** handles log offset tracking and LogStartOffset updates
6. **AlterPartitionManager** communicates ISR changes to the controller
